Here are some fun facts about Australia: - The country is home to more than 60 species of kangaroos. - Australia has the world's longest fence, known as the Dingo Fence, which stretches over 5,600 kilometers.

- The Great Barrier Reef is the largest coral reef system in the world and can be seen from space. - Australia is the only continent without an active volcano.

- The country has over 10,000 beaches, more than any other country in the world. - The Australians once lost a war against emus in 1932, known as the Great Emu War. - The world's largest cattle station, Anna Creek Station, is located in Australia and is larger than the entire country of Portugal.
