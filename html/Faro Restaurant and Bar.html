The Faro Restaurant and Bar is a dining establishment located in the heart of Sydney, Australia. The opening hours are Monday to Saturday from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM, and Sunday from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM. It is known for its contemporary Australian cuisine and vibrant atmosphere. The restaurant features a diverse menu that highlights fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced from local suppliers. Guests can enjoy a range of dishes, including seafood, grilled meats, and vegetarian options, all expertly prepared by the talented culinary team. The Faro Restaurant and Bar also boasts an extensive wine list, showcasing the best of Australian wines. Whether you're looking for a casual meal or a special dining experience, Faro is the perfect destination.
