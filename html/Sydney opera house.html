Opening hours: Monday - Friday: 09:00 - 17:00 Saturday - Sunday: 10:00 - 16:00 Adults pay $43 for a standard tour, while children are charged $23. There are also concession prices at $33 and family packages available. If you want to see a show, ticket prices vary depending on the performance and seating choice. The Sydney Opera House is a world-renowned performing arts center located in Sydney, Australia. It is famous for its unique architectural design, featuring a series of large, white sail-shaped shells.
