Lunetta Trattoria is an Italian restaurant located in Sydney, Australia. It is renowned for its authentic Italian cuisine, featuring a variety of traditional dishes such as handmade pasta. The restaurant offers a cozy and inviting atmosphere, perfect for both casual dining and special occasions. The menu includes a selection of appetizers, main courses, and desserts, all prepared with fresh ingredients.
