Gimlet at Cavendish House is a restaurant in Melbourne, Australia.

It is known for its modern Australian cuisine and its focus on using fresh, local ingredients. The restaurant is located in the historic Cavendish House building, which adds to its charm and ambiance. The menu at Gimlet features a variety of dishes that showcase the diverse flavors of Australia, including seafood, meats, and vegetarian options.

Its opening hours are Sun-Thu noon-10pm; Fri-Sat noon-1am For a four course menu, the price is $160 per person.
